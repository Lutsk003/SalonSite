What We Do

Located in the heart of Roseville, on a NE side of the Rosedale Mall, Stephen's Hair Salon is a modern beauty salon offering a variety of hair services.

Every client is special to us and our goal is to help find a true beauty and add a little bit of spark in everyday life full of worries.

Our services and specialties include:

  • Vibrant, customized hair color
  • Goergeous highlights
  • Balayage or Color melt designed for you
  • Brazilian Blowout
  • Keratin treatments
  • Wedding, Prom, and other special occasions

We have flexible appointments to meet the needs of our busy clients. We're here when you need us, and late nights by appointment. And we will try our best to fit you in for last minute, unexpected events. We'll teach you how to keep your hair looking fabulous at home, how to care for your curls or your sleek, straight look and what products are the right ones for you. We only use top of the line professional high quality products for our services.

Please follow us on a Facebook for a special promotions and weekly deals!
